Media

Background of The Ministry of Mass Media Sri Lanka

Raising public awareness on programmes related to state policies implemented at national level and the role of the institutions affiliated to the Ministry. Facilitating to upgrade professionalism of journalists

Functions

  • Managing Applications for TV, Radio, Cable, Satellite/IPTV
  • Managing Licensing of Media Institutions
  • Managing Rules & Regulations of Media
  • Implementation of the scholarship programme for journalists.
  • Providing credit facilities for journalists to purchase media equipment.
  • Conducting press briefings and publicity activities on projects implemented by various Ministries,
  • Departments and other state institutions.
  • Organization of training workshops for journalists.
  • Issuing press releases for print and electronic media
  • Implementation of publicity activities of affiliated institutions of the Ministry of Parliamentary Reforms and Mass Media.
  • Media coordination and publicizing projects implemented by the Ministry for journalists.
  • Update and maintain the official website of the Ministry tri-lingually.
  • Implementation of publicity activities of affiliated institutions of the Ministry of Finance and Mass Media.
  • Preparing investigative reports containing important news published daily in print media and archiving such articles.
  • Selection and archiving of economically, politically, socially significant newspaper articles on current issues.
  • Organization of social gatherings for journalists.

Vision

“A well – informed, pluralistic and dynamic society”

Mission

“Formulating, Facilitating, Implementing, Monitoring and Evaluating policies and strategies in order to establish a people friendly, development – oriented, free and responsible Sri Lankan media culture”
