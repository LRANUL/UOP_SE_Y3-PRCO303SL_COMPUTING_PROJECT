Legal Background of the Department for Registrations of Persons

Department for Registration of Persons is established to implement the provisions of the Registration of Persons Act No 32 of 1968. This Act is in operation from April 5, 1991. Legal background of the Department for Registration of Persons is provided through the following Regulations, Orders and Announcements.

Acts

  • Registration of Persons Act No 32 of 1968
  • Registration of Persons Amendment Act No 28 of 1971
  • Registration of Persons Amendment Act No 37 f 1971
  • Registration of Persons Amendment Act No 11 of 1981
  • Registration of Persons Amendment Act No 8 of 2016

Authorities, duties and functions of the Department for Registration of Persons

  1. Registration of specified persons living in Sri Lanka
  2. Issuing of identity cards to such registered persons
  3. Performance of duties and functions related to above two subjects
